    Interaction Design, Installation, 2019

    We’ve designed a platform that provides people with an opportunity to engage and understand the emotions of strangers in a subtler way.
    A certain emotion is informationized to be recorded and stored, and then presented to the audience through videos or spacial experience. We can imagine that this is an emotion bank. People can put their “emotions” into the “bank” or view other saved in the “bank”, leading to the emotional interaction among strangers.
